About Kinetic Training Org

Our Story
Kinetic Training Org began in 2022 as a small community project to help teenagers get moving in a world full of screens. What started as simple weekend group activities soon turned into a full program that blends fitness, mental wellness, and education, helping young people stay active and healthy.

Our Mission
Our mission is to support adolescents in their day-to-day lives through programs focused on fitness and personal development. We strive to help youth develop a mindset that benefits both themselves and their communities.

Meet Our Founder
Meet Kent M. Tumol, a curious student of the PSHS that advocates for both physical and mental health. He is dedicated to enhancing the quality of life of this generation and future generations to come.
